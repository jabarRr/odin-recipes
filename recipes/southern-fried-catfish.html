Southern Fried Catfish

Ingredients

  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup water
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound catfish fillets, cut in strips
  • 1 ½ cups fine cornmeal
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning, such as Old Bay™
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for deep frying

How to Cook:

  1. Mix buttermilk, water, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Pour mixture into a flat pan large enough to hold fillets.
  2. Arrange fillets in a single layer in the pan, turning to coat each side. Set aside to marinate.
  3. Combine cornmeal, flour, and seafood seasoning in a 2-gallon resealable plastic bag.
  4. Add fillets to the bag, a few at a time, and tumble gently until evenly coated.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C). Fry fillets in hot oil until crisp and golden brown, about 3 minutes. Work in batches to avoid overcrowding, so fillets have room to brown properly.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
